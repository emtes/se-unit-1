Beyonce

Queen B who gave us many bops

Here is a timeline of Beyonce's life and career:

  • 1981 - Born in Houston, Texas
  • 1989 - Beyonce and three other girls enter Star Search, the most popular talent TV show at the time
  • 1996 - Music group changes name and signs a contract with Columbia Records
  • 2003 - Records first solo featured in Jay Z's "03 Bonnie and Clyde"
"I get nervous when I don't get nervous. If I'm nervous I know I'm going to have a good show." -- Beyonce
